Privacy Policy

AIChatOCR ("the App") values user privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is handled when using the App.

1. Information We Collect
   - Chat history: Stored on device.
   - Imported documents / exported files: Stored on device.
   - Settings (e.g., consent flags): Stored on device.
   - We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name or email address).

2. Purposes of Use
   - Chat history: To continue displaying chats and improve convenience.
   - Imported documents / exported files: To provide functionality and enable user-initiated sharing.
   - Settings (e.g., consent flags): To retain app settings.

3. Collection Methods
   - All processing occurs on device. AI and OCR functions run on device, and neither text nor images are transmitted externally.

4. Notice and Consent
   - Consent to the Terms of Service is obtained at first launch. The Privacy Policy can be reviewed at any time from Settings.

5. External Transmission, Third-Party Provision, and Data Collection Modules
   - We do not use analytics or tracking modules.
   - Except as required by law, we do not provide user information to third parties.

6. Contact
   - For inquiries about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the "Support Contact" listed on the App Store.

7. Revisions
   - This Policy may be revised as necessary, and any changes will be announced on this page.

8. Effective/Updated Date

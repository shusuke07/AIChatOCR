Boyoyon Sim.

A handy companion for Mystery Dungeon runs.
Quickly test “Boyoyon wall” bounce patterns.
Recreate wall layouts and quickly find throws that land back at your feet!

What it does

Boyoyon Sim. helps you simulate throw patterns for “Boyoyon wall” on your phone.
Recreate room size and wall placement, then list throws (start position & direction) and trajectories that meet your conditions.

  • Easy wall editing: tap/drag to place walls, fill modes, bulk clear/fill for inside area
  • Readable results: trajectories on a grid, direction filters, sorting, and “lands at feet only” filtering
  • Safer checks: option to exclude patterns that go out of bounds
  • Smooth mobile UI: pinch to zoom and touch-friendly controls
